## 2026-07-20 (fourth entry — two reservation leaks closed)

Done:
- Closed a stranded-reservation leak in /pay-and-call. An unexpected throw
  (a DB blip inside markSigned, say) escaped past the outcome handling, so the
  row stayed `reserved` forever: every retry of that subtask got IN_FLIGHT and

  row older than RESERVATION_STALE_AFTER_SECONDS (default 900s, must exceed the

  reclaimed regardless of age — that money may already be gone.

  double-counting the budget, and a `signed` row aged thirty days still returns
  REQUIRES_HUMAN.

## 2026-07-20 (fifth entry — decimal-scale bug found in my own diff)

Done:
- Bug sweep over the branch turned up a real money bug I had introduced. The cap
  checks compare the vendor's price against the caller's `max_amount` as raw
  base-unit integers, but that comparison is only meaningful when both are on the
  same decimal scale. A vendor pricing in an 18-decimal token against a 6-decimal
  `max_amount` would have sailed straight through every cap: 15 units at 18
  decimals is a trillion times 15 units at 6.
- Fix: `SelectedOffer` now carries the vendor's `declaredDecimals` (from
  `accepts[].extra.decimals`, null when the vendor declares none). If the vendor
  declares a scale that differs from the caller's, /pay-and-call refuses BEFORE
  the cap check and before signing. It does not convert — converting would mean
  deciding on the caller's behalf what they meant to authorise.
- The receipt now records `asset` (what the vendor named) and `declared_decimals`
  (null when unverified), so `amount.decimals` is no longer silently presented as
  a verified fact when it is only the caller's assumption.
- Verified by negative control: stubbing the guard to `if (false)` makes the new
  test fail with a completed PAYMENT_FAILED instead of a refusal.

Also fixed (F2 lane, gateway): the payment gate verified against a freshly-read
quote and then `execute` re-read it, so a quote expiring in that window left the
caller charged with no task. The verified quote is now passed through to the
insert instead of being re-fetched.
