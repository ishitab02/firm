## Google-form product summary

The Firm is an autonomous prime contractor for the OKX agent economy. A buyer
provides a goal and budget; The Firm checks candidate endpoints and live x402
prices before signing, enforces spend caps, hires specialist agents, validates
their work, replaces failures while preserving the quoted price, and returns one
deliverable with a costed provenance receipt.

The entry is live at `https://firm-gateway.fly.dev/`. Its buyer path has made two
real outbound x402 payments on X Layer to OKLink Onchain Data Explorer #2023,
including one full graph-to-vendor run; a real retry reused the recorded receipt
instead of paying twice. These transactions are procurement costs, not revenue.

The product is grounded in original, reproducible marketplace research. Our
218-agent, ten-query search snapshot found 95 endpoint-bearing agents on July
21. Of those, 41 failed the unpaid preflight: nine were unreachable and 32
returned unusable HTTP responses. Seven returned 200 without a challenge; five
of those advertised nonzero fees. Five live prices exceeded their listings. The
demo clearly labels its live unpaid checks, historical real transactions, and
simulated controlled-failure sequence.
